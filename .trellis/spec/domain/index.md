## Current Domain Priorities

The project currently centers on:

- Student workbench
- Teacher intervention and review
- Admin governance and audit
- Bloom-level progression
- Challenge-driven upgrades
- Reward-bearing events for future training data

Anything that pulls the product back toward a generic LMS shell, a course portal, or a generic chat platform is out of scope unless the PRD is explicitly changed.
